# Food Engines Notice

Food Engines is an original static browser game created for Summer into AI Week 2: Independence Engines.

The project intentionally does not use FarmVille, Stardew Valley, Hay Day, Farm Story, or other commercial game names, art, crop assets, UI, sounds, characters, logos, monetization systems, or social mechanics. The broad reference category is casual farm-grid games, but the implementation, copy, and visuals are original.

All runtime visuals are generated with HTML/CSS and simple procedural shapes. No external copyrighted art or audio assets are included.
